“All the flowers of the tomorrows are in the seeds of today.”

Poetry

Just like the clouds over the earth,
so is God omnipresent.
Just like flowers grow from mud,
so did God created us from same.
like flowers need purning for growth,
so do we from trials & temptations.
just like flowers need rain to bloom,
so will man need conversion & motivation.
